## 🧭 My Data Journey

It started with a **Bachelor's degree in Statistics**.
That's where I learned to interpret data — not just calculate it.
Data isn't a number. It's a context, a decision, a story with stakes.

I deepened that foundation through self-study. But the experience that changed how I *see* data was joining the
**Bangkit Academy Machine Learning Cohort (2023–2024)**.
A full year of building, breaking, and rebuilding models — it showed me just how much
data shapes real-world outcomes.

After graduating, I worked as a **Data Analyst at ISSED**, a research consultancy in Banda Aceh.
I dealt with data from academic researchers, government agencies, and policy teams —
cleaned over 30,000 rows, built regression and clustering models, and translated findings
into recommendations actual decision-makers could use.

Now I'm based in **South Jakarta**, working as a **Technical Writer at CBNCloud**.
Different title. Same instinct: find what's unclear, figure out why, fix it.
I'm building toward a **Business Analyst role** — intentionally, project by project.
